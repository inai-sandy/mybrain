1 · The big idea

Tasks & Activity isn't a to-do list. It's a daily loop designed so the app gradually understands who you are — where your time goes, what you finish, what you avoid, and how you feel about your days.

🌅 Morning — the plan

You dump your brain and the AI turns the mess into a clean, prioritized task list.

🌙 Night — the understanding

You tell the story of your day — the human context tasks alone can't give.

Everything in between is captured automatically and woven into a daily story and, over time, an honest portrait of you. Run it all inside the app or entirely from Telegram.

3 · Getting started

  1. Open mybrain.1site.ai and sign in.
  2. Tap Tasks in the menu — this is your Today screen.
  3. Tap 🧠 Dump my brain, type or speak what's on your mind, then Make my tasks.
  4. (Recommended) Connect Telegram — see §9.

4 · The Today screen

Your daily cockpit:

  • 🧠 Dump my brain — type or tap the mic to speak. The AI turns rambling sentences into concrete tasks, removes duplicates, guesses a category, priority & time estimate, pins your top 1–3 must-dos ⭐️, and asks one clarifying question if your dump is too vague.
  • ＋ Add task — add a single task by hand.
  • Your task list — full search, filters, sorting, and a progress bar.
  • Tap the circle to finish a task — it asks "how long did it really take?" in one tap.

5 · Understanding a task

FieldMeaning
TitleWhat to do.
CategoryA bucket the AI learns (Beakn, Admin, Health…).
PriorityHigh / Medium / Low.
⭐️ PinnedOne of your 1–3 must-dos for the day.
EstimateThe AI's guess at how long it'll take.
ActualWhat it really took (you tap this when you finish).
Reminders0–4 per task; the AI picks smart times. Delivered via Telegram.
Carry-over: unfinished tasks roll to tomorrow automatically. Keep carrying the same one and it gets flagged "carried 3d" in red — one of the most honest signals about you, and the coach will call it out.

6 · The nightly story & quick notes

  • 🌙 Tonight's story — mandatory by design. A free narrative of your day: problems, wins, what happened. Type or speak it and tap a mood. This is the emotional layer that lets the app truly understand you — and it shapes tomorrow's priorities.
  • 📝 Quick notes — speak or type little notes through the day; they land in your timeline.

7 · The Activity section

Four tabs:

🗓 Day

Date navigator, stat cards (done / time / follow-through), the AI day-summary (auto-written 9:30 PM, saved to your memory), your story, and an auto-captured timeline of everything you did that day.

📊 Insights

🔥 dump streak, follow-through %, where your time went (by category), estimate vs reality, and a tasks-per-day chart. 7 / 30 / 90 days.

📆 Calendar

A GitHub-style heatmap — darker = more done. Tap any day to open it.

👤 Me — your portrait & Validate

Locked until 10 real days, then an honest, evidence-grounded portrait of you, refreshed every 3 days. Validate each insight with ✓ correct / ✗ not me — the app respects your feedback forever, so it sharpens and never drifts.

8 · The personality engine

The real point of the whole feature. Every 3 days an AI "coach" reads across your tasks, stories, moods, and activity, cross-checks what you planned vs what you did vs how you felt, and builds a validated picture of you — deliberately honest, not flattering, so you see yourself clearly. Runs on Claude Sonnet by default.

10 · Settings worth knowing

  • Models — the Tasks AI model runs the whole engine. Defaults to Claude Sonnet; pick any OpenAI or Anthropic model.
  • Prompts — read and edit the exact instructions the AI follows for every feature. Save, or Reset to default, any time.
  • Integrations — your API keys (OpenRouter, Anthropic, OpenAI for voice, Telegram).
  • Sync → Telegram — connection status, connect / re-register, unlink.

11 · Tips

  • Dump fast and messy. Don't organize as you type — that's the AI's job.
  • Always log the real time when you finish a task — that's what powers the time insights.
  • Tell the story even on bad days. The rough days teach the app the most.
  • Use Telegram on busy days — the days you won't open the app are the most revealing.
  • Validate your portrait — a few ✓/✗ taps make it sharper every cycle.
  • Rest days are fine. /skip means the streak never becomes a stick.

12 · Quick FAQ

Will syncing my memory create duplicates?
No — everything the app generates is stamped and skipped on sync.
What if my brain-dump is vague?
The AI asks one clarifying question instead of inventing tasks.
Can I edit what the AI says?
Yes — Settings → Prompts lets you rewrite any instruction, with a Reset button.
When does the personality portrait appear?
After 10 active days, then it refreshes every 3 days.
Do I have to use Telegram?
No — everything works in the app. Telegram just makes it effortless on the go.
